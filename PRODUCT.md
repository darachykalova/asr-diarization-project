# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Internal staff only, no public or client-facing surface. Two roles: **moderators**, who triage audio uploads and transcripts, review speaker identities, and review anti-scam call verdicts; and **super_admins**, who do everything moderators do plus manage users, platform settings, and the audit log. Both roles work in this panel routinely as part of their day-to-day job, not occasionally.

## Product Purpose

The admin console for an audio intelligence platform (ASR, speaker diarization, cross-recording speaker ID, transcript search, and a real-time anti-scam voice call agent). It exists so staff can browse and search processed audio and transcripts, manage speaker identities, review and act on flagged scam calls, and administer the platform (users, settings, audit log) — all from one panel, without touching the API directly.

## Positioning

The one place staff go to review every processed call and transcript and catch scam patterns as they happen, while also administering the platform itself — not a general-purpose audio archive and not a marketing surface.

## Brand Personality

Strict, fast, business-like. The tool that flags manipulation and urgency in scam calls should not itself lean on urgency or manipulation in its own interface — no playful flourishes, no attention-grabbing gimmicks. Confidence comes from clarity and speed, not decoration.

## Anti-references

Should not read as a playful or bright SaaS marketing landing page — no gradient hero sections, no bouncy/celebratory motion, no oversized display typography. This is a working tool, not a pitch.

## Design Principles

- **Speed over decoration.** Moderators triage flagged calls under time pressure; every screen should get out of the way of that task rather than dress it up.
- **Practice what it detects.** The product's whole value is catching manipulative, urgency-driven patterns in scam calls — its own UI should never use those same tricks on the people using it.
- **One tool, two audiences.** The same surfaces serve day-to-day moderation and platform administration equally; neither should crowd out or outrank the other.
- **Boring is a feature.** Consistency and predictability (same place, same behavior, every time) matter more than novelty for staff who use this every day.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Clear text contrast and respect for `prefers-reduced-motion` (movement dropped, opacity/color feedback kept) are expected throughout. No specific WCAG conformance level has been adopted.
